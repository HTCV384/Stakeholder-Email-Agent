
## Overview

The enhanced email style system supports three distinct modes of email generation, providing flexibility from fully automated AI generation to complete user control.

## Three Generation Modes

### Mode 1: AI-Generated Styles
**Description:** Pre-defined style prompts that guide the AI to generate emails in specific tones and formats.

**Characteristics:**
- No user editing required
- Consistent output based on style guidelines
- AI interprets and applies the style
- Multiple flavors available

**Style Flavors:**

1. **Professional to Healthcare Professional**
   - Formal, evidence-based tone
   - Clinical terminology
   - Focus on patient outcomes and compliance
   - Structured format with clear sections

2. **Casual Bro-y**
   - Conversational, direct language
   - Short sentences and fragments
   - Problem-focused with urgency
   - Uses "you/your" extensively
   - Minimal formality

3. **Technical and Direct**
   - Focus on specifications and capabilities
   - Data-driven language
   - Clear technical details
   - Solution-oriented

4. **Executive Brief**
   - High-level strategic focus
   - Business impact emphasis
   - Concise and to-the-point
   - ROI and value proposition

5. **Consultative**
   - Problem-solving approach
   - Collaborative tone
   - Question-based engagement
   - Partnership focus

---

### Mode 2: User-Editable Templates
**Description:** Pre-structured email templates with placeholders that users can customize before generation.

**Characteristics:**
- Template structure provided
- User fills in key sections
- AI enhances with stakeholder context
- Balance of control and automation

**Template Structure:**
```
Subject: [USER_EDITABLE]

Opening Hook: [USER_EDITABLE]

Context/Problem Statement:
[AI_GENERATED from stakeholder research]

Your Solution/Value Proposition: [USER_EDITABLE]

Specific Application to Stakeholder:
[AI_GENERATED from stakeholder context]

Call to Action: [USER_EDITABLE]

Closing: [USER_EDITABLE]
```

**Example Template Types:**
1. Problem-Solution Template
2. Case Study Template
3. Partnership Proposal Template
4. Product Introduction Template
5. Follow-up Template

---
